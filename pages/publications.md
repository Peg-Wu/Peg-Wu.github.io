### <b style="color: purple">Research Interests</b>

My  research focuses on **AI for Biology**, with a specialization in the **AI Virtual Cell (AIVC)**. My core work involves:

- **Building foundation models using multi-modal data.**
- **Predicting single-cell responses to perturbations.**
- **Engineering agentic AIVCs to automate and accelerate biological discovery.**

{: .box-note}
"VCs must work across biological scales, over time, and across data modalities and should help reveal the programming language of cellular systems and provide an interface to use it for engineering purposes." -- [***Cell 2024***](https://www.cell.com/cell/fulltext/S0092-8674(24)01332-1)
